Safe Kid

About This Project

One of my senior capstone projects, Safe Kid is an app we created for a local group that works with autistic children. The app is designed to use repetition and learning techniques to help children learn important information such as their address, emergency contacts, home number, and more. Created in Unity with the intention of publishing onto iOS, the application earned an award at KSU's semi-annual "C Day" showcase.

Technologies used:C#, Unity Game Engine
