Hobbies

Model Railroading

A lifelong fan of railroads, I picked up this wonderful hobby from my grandfather. Currently, I have a small N scale layout in my home, and am planning for a classic basement HO layout set in transition era Appalachia.

Tabletop Gaming

I've been playing card games and ttrpgs regularly since I was a teenager, and have met so many lifelong friends doing so. I actively play Magic: The Gathering and Yugioh on the TCG side, and am watching the upcoming Cyberpunk TCG with great interest. On the TTRPG Side, I've been both player and DM. Currently alternating between running Cyberpunk Red, and playing in DnD 5e.

Reading

I've been an avid reader all my life, and have a particular love of fantasy. Currently working my way through the Discworld series, having recently finished the stormlight archives. My absolute favorite series is the Powder Mage trilogy by Brian McClellan, as well as the sequel trilogy Gods of Blood and Powder. Other books that come to mind include the Mistborn Trilogy, and the five book "Shadow Campaigns" by Django Wexler.

Video Games

I've never met a Fire Emblem entry I didn't like. My entire family are gamers, and we enjoy playing together. With a backlog a hundred and fifty titles long, you'd THINK I'd work on that rather than dive into another Stellaris run.... but alas!
